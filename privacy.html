Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

  • Your email address (via Google login) for account management
  • GDB backtrace and source file snippets you submit for analysis
  • Usage count (number of analyses performed)

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not store your full source code or codebase
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use your crashes to train AI models

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide crash analysis results via Claude AI (Anthropic)
  • To track your free analysis usage limit
  • To improve analysis accuracy through anonymised crash patterns

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL). Crash data is processed via AWS Lambda and sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Source files are never stored permanently.

Third Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude — AI analysis engine
  • Supabase — Authentication and database
  • AWS Lambda — Secure backend processing
  • Lemon Squeezy — Payment processing

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing pillaydevendra3@gmail.com.
